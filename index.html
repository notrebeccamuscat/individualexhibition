What is Conscious?

Conscious. is a collaborative exhibition that aims to raise awareness. That's it. The exhibition features artworks from three artists, each of whom have created a collection of artworks that explore the theme of awareness, mindfulness and wellbeing. The exhibition is a celebration of the arts and the therapeutic benefits of artistic expression.

FEATURED ARTISTS: REBECCA MUSCAT // ANISIA PETRUCCELLI // ANNA CHIARA PESSIA

Collaborating for collective consciousness.

Why do we do what we do?

Discover the profound impact of life's little things, elevating the ordinary into extraordinary expressions of beauty. Through captivating visual storytelling, every captured moment unveils a deeper understanding of our world. In the enigmatic realm of "Conscious," simplicity takes center stage, the art of simply being, a potent testament to the interconnectedness of all things. Join us in this intersection where creativity converges with consciousness, unlocking enchantment within the ordinary.

Meet the Makers

The hands and hearts behind conscious. In today's fast-paced world, it's easy to get caught up in the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Embark on a mesmerizing journey with "Conscious," a collaborative exhibition by three visionary women.

👉 Anna 👈

Anna Chiara was born on the 16th August of 2002 in Italy. She specializes in illustration but enjoys documenting her adventures through striking interpretations of her travels.

👉 Becca 👈

Becca is an artist, writer and designer based in Malta. She hates the colour orange and her favourite book is Notes from Under the Floorboards by Fyodor Dostoevsky.

👉 Anisia 👈

Anisia is a multimedia artist and photographer based in Malta. Her work delves into themes of collective consciousness, subtlety in awareness, and being.
